Question - 
A boy has 15 Rupees,
    1 Rupee = 1 Chocolate and
    3 Wrappers of Chocolate = 1 Chocolate
How much Maximum Chocolate it can buy from Shop?

Step 1 :- 
Purchase 15 Rupees = 15 Chocolate

Step 2 :-
15 Wrappers / 3 = 5 Chocolate

Step 3 :- 
5 Wrappers / 3 = 1 Chocolate
+2 Wrappers Remaing

Total Wrappers = 2 + 1 = 3

Step 4 :- 
3 Wrappers / 3 = 1 Chocolate

Step 5 :- 
15 + 5 + 1 + 1
= 22
